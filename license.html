Security Training

Survivors Protective Services offers In-class Security Guard training courses from our location in Malton. Who can get a license?
To be eligible for a license:
 * You must be 18 years of age or older.
* You must be eligible to work in Canada.
* You must have a clean criminal record.
Basic Training and Testing Requirements
Before you apply for a license, you must:
1.Take the security guard or private investigator basic training course.
2.Pass the security guard or private investigator basic test.
After successful completion of these courses you will receive:
 A Security Guard Training Completion Certificate
A Red Cross Emergency First Aid & CPR Level C Certificate.
You will then be able to book your licensing exam with the Ministry at www.ontariosecuritytesting.com, once you pass then you can apply for a Security Guard License.
*We also assist in applying for your license.
The In-class training program is taught in different modules. Each module is usually composed of Video Lectures, Class Notes / PowerPoint slides, and Quizzes and Assignments. The student is required to follow all modules and units in sequence.
