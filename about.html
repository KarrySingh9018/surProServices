About Us

About Us

Survivors Protective Services is a proven, well established security guard company who strives for excellent customer satisfaction and is committed to the highest level of security. It is our intention to gain your business today and maintain it for a long period of time using a simple formula of great service, great communication and outstanding security.
Customer-Focused Solutions:
Survivors Protective Services is in the business of taking care of your business. Just like you, we built our business one customer at a time while focusing on high quality of service and ensuring 100% client satisfaction as the corner stone of our business.
By providing customer-focused solutions, Survivors Protective Services continues to be a leader in bringing value to clients as their preferred total security solutions partner.
Tailor Made Security Services:
Our security solutions can be tailored to your organizations needs and as such, our approach to managing your security is as flexible as your requirements dictate. We have one key goal in mind: to provide you with an all-encompassing security solutions that allow your organization to operate efficiently, effectively and safely.
Availabilities
We provide security guard services across Ontario to Individuals, institutions and companies, big and small.
Our team is always there ready to serve you in times of crisis and with a short notice.
For Brampton, Mississauga and all surrounding cities we can have guards on site within the hour.
For Toronto and for all GTA we can have guards on site within 2 hours.
Our Security Guards:
Each and every guard is bonded, licensed and certified by the Ontario ministry of community, safety and correctional services to meet the training and testing regulations as per security and investigative act 2005 (PSISA).
Our guards are CPR & first aid certified.
