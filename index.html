About Us

Survivors Protective Services is a proven, well established security guard company who strives for excellent customer satisfaction and is committed to the highest level of security.

  • Construction Security

    Whether it’s a manufacturing plant, distribution warehouse, financial institution or a distillery, our highly trained Security professionals are fully trained and certified to secure the premises. We provide excellent services at competitive rates.

  • Condomiums Security

    At Survivors Protective Services we understand that our condominium and high rise security professionals must be an integral part of the management team and it is imperative that we will be familiar with the building’s personnel, residents and daily procedures as we are usually the first point of reference.

  • Industrial Security
